About

Who We Are

WACM is the student chapter of ACM-W, ACM's Women in Computing at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. We provide social, educational and outreach activities for women in the Computer Sciences department, in the university, and in the surrounding community.


How to Contact Us

For inquiries about events, collaborations, or workshops, please contact our co-presidents, Carol Sze and Abigale Kim. For questions related to specific committees, please reach out directly to the respective committee chairs.
